# Insurance Quote Conversion Prediction
**High-Dimensional Classification & Customer Behavior Analytics**

## The Mission
To optimize marketing spend and sales funnels by accurately predicting which customers are most likely to purchase an insurance policy after receiving a quote. This enables targeted follow-ups and personalized customer engagement.

## Technical Architecture
* **Data Engineering:** Processed a massive, anonymized dataset with hundreds of features. Handled missing values, encoded categorical variables, and scaled numerical inputs for optimal model performance.
* **Feature Selection:** Identified the most critical drivers of conversion behavior, stripping away noise from the high-dimensional dataset to prevent model overfitting.
* **Predictive Modeling:** Developed and trained advanced classification algorithms to predict binary purchasing outcomes, focusing heavily on maximizing the ROC-AUC score.

## Key Business Insights
* Created a highly scalable predictive pipeline that allows sales teams to prioritize high-probability leads.
* Replaced traditional "spray and pray" marketing tactics with a data-driven, machine learning approach to customer acquisition.
